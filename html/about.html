// About Us //

OUR OBJECTIVE

"Trash To Craft" is a organization who is working to promote recycling awareness and encourage individuals to systematically separate their trash for reuse by earning money. Manages non-biodegradable garbage, such as paper, cardboard, metal cans, clothing, and thermocol, which we collect and turn into useful items.

We offer our services to both households and businesses that have a difficult time getting rid of their trash. Our organization's services focus toward conquering challenges to the environment, using them effectively, and significantly advancing recycling and trash management.

OUR VISION

The development of more effective recycling technology is a top priority of "Trash to Craft" in order to improve sustainability through more intelligent recycling infrastructure. Our goal is to help create a better future where nothing is wasted, the environment is protected, and technology is used to help people to learn better habits. Make use of technology that is simpler, faster, and multitasking.

OUR MISSION

Teach people about the value of the environment, develop the habit of systematic separation of waste in them, and provide technologies that will help the system remain alive.

We offer work to people who needs it. We hold workshops where we teach them how to create handcrafted goods out of waste so that they can earn for themselves.

OUR MOTTO

The goal of our organization is to improve the quality of life in our community. We strongly think that a healthy world and a healthy community go hand in hand, thus in order to reach this aim, we must protect the environment in which we live. The required actions are being taken by "Trash To Craft" to reduce the negative consequences, protect and maintain our natural resources, and create a sustainable environment.

HOW IS TRASH PROCESSED?

Processed in three ways:
SEGREGATION
Sorting of waste into different categories to reuse.
HANDOVER
Handover the sorted waste to our team.
CONVERT
Convert the waste to handmade home decor items.
